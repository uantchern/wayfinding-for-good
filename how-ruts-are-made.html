How RUTs Are Made

We believe that software shouldn't take months to build or weeks to learn. This is our blueprint for discovering and developing Really Useful Things in record time.

1

The "Boring" Trigger

It always starts with a charity worker expressing frustration over a "boring but important" task. Whether it’s digesting changes to the Code of Governance, formatting a newsletter, or processing claims—if it's a repetitive drain on human capital, it's a candidate for a RUT.

2

The 24-Hour Sprint

Unlike traditional software lifecycles, our objective is to conceptualize and deploy a working, single-function tool within 24 hours. We strictly adhere to the MAYA Principle (Most Advanced Yet Acceptable), building clean, focused web-interfaces that act as bridges to powerful Large Language Models.

3

The AI❤️ Vibe Check

Tech needs a soul. Before a RUT is shipped, it goes through our AI❤️（爱） methodology. Is the tone compassionate? Does the system prompt encourage positive "Question Storming"? Is it contextualized for Singapore's unique social sector? If it feels cold and robotic, it gets rebuilt.

4

The 90-Day Deployment

RUTs are not meant to live forever; they are built for 90 days of intensive, high-impact use (perfect for a seasonal campaign or an immediate internal audit). Every RUT is engineered with a standard Last-Mile feature: one-click export to WhatsApp, bringing the AI's output directly into the group chats where real human work happens.
