Finding Clarity

CharityOps is an AI social enterprise. Our mission is to discover Really Useful Things (RUTs) for the Singapore charity sector.

A Really Useful Thing (RUT) is a "digital temp worker" in the form of a mini-AI program. RUTs are used for a specific purpose over a limited period of time, for example, creating an impact report immediately after an event. They appeal to charities because they are cheap and disposable.

We rely on grants, backers, and funders to fuel discovery. RUTs are monetized via sustainable business models - subscription fees and one-time fees. Profits are intentionally reinvested: our primary impact is creating jobs for individuals displaced by AI. Our RUTs discovery team comprise of interns, volunteers, retirees and fractionals.

Drop us a note if you are keen on discovering Really Useful Things that can make a difference for charities. We have roles for volunteers, interns, retirees, storytellers, technologists or people looking to refresh their life.
