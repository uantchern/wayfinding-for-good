Finding Clarity

CharityOps is a for-profit social enterprise founded by Philip Chong and Uantchern Loh.

We function as a pathfinder for the Singapore charity sector, developing RUTs through question storming—navigating the forest by asking the right questions, rather than following a map of assumed answers.

RUTs are impact-first, tech-second "things" discovered using the Project AI❤️爱 approach.

We rely on grants, backers, and funders to fuel discovery. RUTs are monetized via sustainable models, and profits are intentionally reinvested: our primary impact is creating jobs for individuals displaced by AI.

Vibe coding bridges human intention and digital creation in Project AI❤️爱. We prioritize the 'vibe'—care and story—over rigid specs, allowing wayfinding through conversation with AI.

We invite stakeholders to participate in wayfinding sessions or back discovery journeys. Involvement begins with curiosity and the willingness to roll a 13.
