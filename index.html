CharityOps is a Singapore-based social enterprise. We are a team of staff, interns, volunteers, retirees, and fractional experts, working together to discover really useful things.

Using AI, we find and develop Really Useful Things (RUTs) for Singapore charities, including their donors, volunteers, and beneficiaries.

  • What is a RUT? It is a thing built for a specific purpose over a period of time.
  • Why they work: RUTs are appealing because they are cost-effective, fast to deploy, and—most importantly—they are really useful things.
  • Example: Imagine creating an impact report immediately after a charity event ends, instead of weeks later. That's a RUT in action.

Our discovery process is guided by the Project AI❤️爱 mindset: Values-first, tech-second. We believe AI should be human-centered, focusing on Ai (compassion and care). We don't just build software; we solve human problems through experimentation and listening.
