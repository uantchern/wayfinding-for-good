CharityOps is a social enterprise. We use AI to discover Really Useful Things for Singapore charities.

We use AI to discover and develop Really Useful Things (RUTs) that handle the heavy lifting of daily operations.

By automating the "boring but important" tasks, we give you back the most valuable resource of all: time to spend with your beneficiaries.

We find RUTs across all charity functions:

  • Governance & Compliance: Automated board assessments and risk tracking.
  • Finance & Admin: Faster claims processing and simple bookkeeping.
  • Fundraising & Grants: Grant summaries and donor engagement tools.
  • Volunteer & HR: Seamless matching, onboarding, and training.
  • Marketing & Research: Quick impact stories and community insights.

Most charities are resource-light but task-heavy, and they are always lacking time. That’s why our RUTs (Really Useful Things) are designed differently. Unlike complex software, a RUT is a single-function tool built for a specific purpose. We can discover and develop one in just 24 hours, making them incredibly fast to deploy.

Each RUT is purpose-driven and built for 90 days of intensive use—perfect for immediate needs like events or seasonal campaigns. They are highly affordable and designed to fit into your daily workflow with WhatsApp integration, so your team can use powerful AI without needing to learn a new system. It’s high-impact tech made simple for the social sector.

Our RUT discovery process is guided by the Project AI❤️爱 mindset: Values-first, tech-second. We believe AI should be human-centered, focusing on Ai (compassion and care). We don't just build software; we solve human problems through experimentation and listening.
